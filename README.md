# Customers_RFM_Anlaysis
**Business Problem:** 
A regional sales company has 3 years of transaction data across 4 product categories and hundreds of customers. The Sales Director is preparing for the quarterly business review and needs to answer three questions the CMO will ask: Which customers are most valuable to the business? Which sales reps are actually driving revenue vs just hitting call targets? And which customers are showing early signs of churn — before they actually leave?
